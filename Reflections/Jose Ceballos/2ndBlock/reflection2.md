### UI desing patterns
UI and design are a fundamental part of software life cycles, because is the first thing the client sees and pretty much our presentation card, even if the system isn´t in development or it is already in production, it creates a satisfactory and comfortable way of presenting information.

As we konw, UI presents some reusable components wich designers use to solve problems in user interface, such as Breadcrumbs, dark desgin, lazy registration, etc. Most of the time, we struggle with some real world problems and break our heads on how to land an idea or a solution, but UI patterns offer prototypes that can be maleable to our desgin. Anothe important aspect of UI desgin patterns is that the don`t solve 100% of the problem, for instance, if i were to ask an IA a problem or some requieremts, they would apply these patterns to our main desgin, but most of the time they would look ¨off¨.

For our project, task #17 was about showing the a more comofortable view of a form, but one of the desgin patterns that we used was the <mark>Clear Primary Actions: Make buttons stand out with color so users know what to do (e.g., “Submit”). You may have to decide which actions take priority.</mark>, so the user can see the submit button.
